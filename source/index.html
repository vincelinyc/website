vince li

product designer

I'm currently Head of Product Design at Managed by Q in New York. Before that I built data management tools at Moat (acquired by Oracle) and worked on several products in Amsterdam.

I started out as a graffiti artist in rural Netherlands where I grew into graphic design. Shortly after I founded Dunkel and Hell (rip) to create artwork for Amsterdam's nightlife where we quickly discovered we wanted to be in nightlife and not just create for it. Schmeck Pony (rip) was born and so the numerous warehouse parties featuring the likes of Kabale und Liebe to Marcus Meinhardt to Marau Picotto.

Through love for technology and a curiosity in the human mind, I moved into digital product design. At Q I exercise all these skills everyday because we operate at the intersection of people, space, and technology. Solving hard problems is where it's at.

Join me at Q.
